How to Make a PB&J Sandwich

  1. Gather ingredients: 2 slices of bread, knife, spoon, peanut butter, jelly, plate
  2. Lay the bread slices on the plate.
  3. Use the knife to spread peanut butter on one side of one slice of bread, to your taste.
  4. Use the spoon to spread jelly on one side of the other slice of bread, to your taste.
  5. Place the two slices of bread together, with the peanut butter side touching the jelly side.
  6. Enjoy!
