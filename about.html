ABOUT

Who am I?

My name is Davita, and I made aliyah to Israel just two months ago. There is a lot more to know about me, but for now, my being an olah chadasha is an integral part of who I am.

I live in a city called Karmiel in the North of Israel, where you can find shops, shuls, parks, doctors offices, and anything else you might need, while still always feeling just a little bit in the middle of nowhere. From just about any vantage point in the city, you can see beautiful, tree-covered hilltops. The sunsets are forever spectacular rainbows, and cows are frequently spotted just off the road.

Before making aliyah, I was a teacher and administrator, working specifically with high school girls. What a dream! It's hard for me to imagine a career more challenging or more rewarding. However, I am excited to dive into web design and development! After summer training in engineering to teach a course, and spending many hours with Arduino code and hardware, in additional to many, many, many hours working on text editing, graphic design, and web design, I am ready to take the next step in my education and career.

Why web design?

My dream is to work with a non-profits or small businesses, and help them grow and increase exposure through web pages that directly meet their goals, and speak to their mission. My career in education has fostered a passion within me to help people, and I can use skills in web design to do just that.

Three Important Things to Know about Me

  1. I love grammar and language!
  2. I can't help being a teacher, even outside of the classroom.
  3. Moving to a new country with a new language - and leaving behind life as you knew it and built it - is really, really hard. I'm excited for this new career path, and also feel like I am stepping off a cliff.
